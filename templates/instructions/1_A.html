Demonstrations

In this section, you will play a simple Lunar Lander game.

The goal is to guide the Lunar Lander to land upright between the goal flags.
Any contact with the surface of the planet will end the game.

There are four actions you can take at any time:

  • Rotate Left (Left Arrow Key)
  • Rotate Right (Right Arrow Key)
  • Use Thrusters (Up Arrow Key)
  • Do Nothing (Gravity will act on the lander)
