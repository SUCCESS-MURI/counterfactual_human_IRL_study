Overview

Chip, the blue triangle, has learned to maximize his score in the three games below!




For each game, Chip will demonstrate the optimal strategy through a series of videos and you will be asked a few questions regarding those demonstrations.

Following the videos, you will have the chance to play the game yourself using your keyboard.

We will introduce each game as we encounter them. But before we dive in, let's head over to a practice game first to get familiar with the keyboard controls and how the games work.
